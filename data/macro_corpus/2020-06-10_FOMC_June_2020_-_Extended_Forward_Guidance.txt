Date: 2020-06-10

Release Date: June 10, 2020

For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT

The coronavirus outbreak is causing tremendous human and economic hardship... Financial conditions have improved, in part reflecting policy measures... The Committee decided to maintain the target range for the federal funds rate at 0 to 1/4 percent. The Committee expects to maintain this target range until it is confident that the economy has weathered recent events...

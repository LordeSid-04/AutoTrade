Date: 2022-12-14

Release Date: December 14, 2022

For release at 2:00 p.m. EST

Recent indicators point to modest growth in spending and production... Inflation remains elevated... The Committee decided to raise the target range for the federal funds rate to 4-1/4 to 4-1/2 percent. The Committee anticipates that ongoing increases in the target range will be appropriate in order to attain a stance of monetary policy that is sufficiently restrictive to return inflation to 2 percent over time.

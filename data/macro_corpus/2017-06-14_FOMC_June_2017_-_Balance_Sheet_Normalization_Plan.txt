Date: 2017-06-14

Release Date: June 14, 2017

For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT

Information received since the Federal Open Market Committee met in May indicates that the labor market has continued to strengthen... In view of realized and expected labor market conditions and inflation, the Committee decided to raise the target range for the federal funds rate to 1 to 1-1/4 percent. The Committee also expects to begin implementing a balance sheet normalization program this year...

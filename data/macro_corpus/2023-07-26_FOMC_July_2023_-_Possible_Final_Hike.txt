Date: 2023-07-26

Release Date: July 26, 2023

For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT

Recent indicators suggest that economic activity has been expanding at a moderate pace. Job gains have been robust in recent months... Inflation remains elevated. The U.S. banking system is sound and resilient... The Committee decided to raise the target range for the federal funds rate to 5-1/4 to 5-1/2 percent. The Committee will continue to assess additional information and its implications for monetary policy.
